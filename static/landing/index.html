Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BrandInsight

How accurate are the trend predictions?

Our trend predictions have an average accuracy rate of 96% based on historical data validation. We use multiple models including Prophet and LSTM neural networks to ensure reliable forecasts.

What data sources do you use?

We aggregate data from multiple sources including Google Trends, Reddit discussions, e-commerce platforms (Amazon, eBay), customer reviews, and news articles to provide comprehensive market insights.

Can I integrate BrandInsight with my existing tools?

Yes, we offer API access for all Professional and Enterprise plans. You can integrate our insights with your CRM, analytics dashboards, or custom applications.

How does the sentiment analysis work?

We use VADER (Valence Aware Dictionary and sEntiment Reasoner), a lexicon and rule-based sentiment analysis tool specifically attuned to sentiments expressed in social media, along with custom models trained on product reviews and customer feedback.
